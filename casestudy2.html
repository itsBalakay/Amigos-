Dropbox Split

Social outings always come with a tricky social dance around how to split the bill. I was tasked with creating a fictional app experience for peer bill splitting as part of a design exercise for Dropbox.

Role

UX Designer

Team

Solo

Timeline

7 Days

Pain points to solve

  1. Lack of transprency

    Users never felt confident they were giving or receiving the right amount of money.

  2. Experience fragmentation

    People need to jump between 3 or more apps to calculate, communicate, and pay.

User sharing how they use Venmo, one of the many stops in the current splitting process.

Getting Started

Dropbox Split was designed to match key design structures from Dropbox’s real apps, recognizing the importance of matching the mental models for onboarding in existing users of their product ecosystem.

Manage your bills easily

The home screen contains a list of the user’s bills. Each item conveys a title, payment progress, and the friends you’ve looped in. These were carefully designed to convey the most relevant information whether the user is a host or a guest using just one structure.
